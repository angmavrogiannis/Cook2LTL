Tear chicken into bite size pcs, mix with soup, chili, salsa and tapioca. Line bottom of crock pot with 3 corn tortillas, torn into bite size pcs. Add in 1/3 of the chicken mix. Sprinkle with 1/3 of the onion and 1/3 of the grated cheese. Repeat layers of tortillas topped with chicken mix, onions and cheese. Cover and cook on low 6 to 8 hrs or possibly high for 3 hrs. Garnish with sliced black olives.
